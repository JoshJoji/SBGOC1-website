Who We Are

Our church is made up of families of young professionals and college students who seek to worship God through the Orthodox tradition. As Christians, it is our duty to uphold our values and spread the fellowship of christianity to others and fellow Christians as well. We also continue to grow our faith towards God by spreading his Word and performing good deeds around the world. Our priest, deacons, and seminarians help us by teaching us the Word of God and shows us how we can grow, both physically and spiritually, while having Him in our lives. Though we do treasure our language and tradition from Kerala, India, we do know it is a necessity to gradually adapt to the environment here, without forgetting our values.

Our Mission

Our mission is to transform the lives of our community to be a reflection of Jesus Christ. Our vision is to accomplish the purpose of being an Orthodox Christian and to be united as a church.
